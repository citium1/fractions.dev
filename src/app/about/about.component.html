About.

Nightmares.io is a personal project by Hampus Lagsten. The site is used as a digital résumé where you can find updated information about Hampus, his projects, competences and availability.

With 8 years of professional experience, mainly in business analysis, product and project management, Hampus excel in leading technical transformation, solution architecture, product development, and diverse product delivery models.
While passionate about software development, especially Angular and web technologies, the primary focus has been concentrated on strategic planning, product roadmap development and risk management, rather than daily coding. Hampus is a generalist with an open-minded approach, high adaptability, and a constant eagerness to learn.
