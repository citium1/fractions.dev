About.

Welcome to nightmares.io, a personal project by Hampus Lagsten. The site is used as a digital résumé where you can find updated information about me, my projects and availability.

I’m Computer Scientist and consultant based in Stockholm, Sweden, with over 8 years of professional experience. Specializing in Technical Transformation, Project Management, Business Analysis and Software Engineering, I’ve had the privilege of working with prominent companies in various sectors, from vehicle manufacturing, Travel Management, Retail and Banking, across multiple countries. Learn more about who I am, what I do, and how I can support your organization.


Current Assignment:

Product Owner of an Enterprise Development Platform at one of the largest banks in the Scandinavian and Baltic regions.

With 8 years of professional experience, and primary focus on project management and business analysis, I excel in driving technical transformation, designing solution architecture and leading product development and various forms of product delivery, such as Platform as a Service to fully managed Software as a Service. Apart from being hands-on in technical implementations, I’ve focused on strategic planning, developing product roadmaps and leading risk management. Although passionate about software development, particularly in Angular and web technologies, I prioritize leading development rather than daily coding. I see myself as a generalist, with an open minded approach, high adaptability and constant eagerness to learn.

Experience

Swedbank AB, Stockholm Sweden | Product Owner

2021 - PRESENT

As the Product Owner of Swedbank’s Frontend Development Platform, I have successfully led its transformation into a Group-wide Strategic Development Platform, now utilized by approximately 200 software developers and 25 applications, including the Swedish and Baltic Internet Bank Applications. The product ownership also includes leading a highly regarded core team at the bank, central in setting the future for the organization's technical possibilities.


Key Achievements:
  • Led the implementation of an Evergreen approach to the platform’s technology stack, resulting in an enterprise-level platform with minimal to zero technical debt, enabling the opportunity to work with the latest technologies for me, my team and hundreds of Software Engineers.
  • Made strategic decisions to modernize the toolchain and processes for application delivery, transitioning from 12 centrally managed production deployments per year to fully automated continuous delivery, while maintaining compliance in a highly regulated market.
  • Adopted cloud transformation strategies, utilizing cloud services in multiple areas to transition the platform from static to highly dynamic and scalable, achieving cost efficiency and improved performance.
